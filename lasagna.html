lasagna

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish that combines layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and gooey cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Egg (beaten)
  • Part-Skim Ricotta Cheese
  • Fresh Parsley (chopped)
  • KRAFT Shredded Low-Moisture Part-Skim Mozzarella Cheese
  • KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • CLASSICO Tomato And Basil Pasta Sauce
  • water
  • lasagna noodles(uncoocked)

steps

  1. Brown the ground beef and mix it with the tomato sauce.
  2. Layer the meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and noodles in a baking dish.
  3. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
  4. Bake until bubbly and golden brown
