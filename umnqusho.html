Umnqusho

Umngqusho, also known as “samp and beans,” is a traditional South African dish that has been enjoyed for generations. It is a hearty and flavorful combination of corn kernels (samp) and dried sugar beans

Ingredients

  • 500 g samp (well rinsed)
  • 800 g dried sugar beans (well rinsed)
  • Water (to cover)
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

steps

  1. Place the samp and beans together in a pot with water. Cover and bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat and cook until soft for at least 2 hours. Do not stir during cooking to avoid excessive starchiness.
  2. Check the pot occasionally to ensure the mixture does not stick to the bottom. Add water if needed.
  3. After about an hour, add the beef stock cubes. Allow it to simmer until everything is soft.
  4. season with salt and pepper
  5. Serve Umngqusho with umleqwa (free-range chicken) or lamb curry for the ultimate taste experience.
